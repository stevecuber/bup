Oplan Ligtas Biyahe: Undas 2013

    Pursuant to the Memorandum of DOTC Undersecretary Eduardo SI Oban, Jr. dated 10 October 2013 regarding “Oplan Ligtas Biyahe: Undas 2013,” LTO Law Enforcement Service (LES) Dir. Mohammad Yusoph Lamping assigned sufficient FED law enforcers and IID/NMVIC inspectors along the Northbound and Southbound of EDSA to provide immediate and appropriate response and assistance to motorists and commuters passing through their areas of responsibilities. Oplan "Hubaran" On Advertisements Posted on Public Utility Buses (PUBs)

    To continously intensify operations against the advertisements posted on Public Utility Buses as well as motor vehicles violating traffic rules and regulations at EDSA and in all other Metro Manila areas.
